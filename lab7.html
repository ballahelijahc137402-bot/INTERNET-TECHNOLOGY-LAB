📚 Stream and Subject Directory

  1. 🔬 Science Stream
    1. Physics

      📘 Physics

      Stream: Science

      Description: Physics is the study of matter, energy, and the interaction between them.

      Topics: Mechanics, Optics, Thermodynamics, Electricity, Magnetism

      Credits: 4

    2. Chemistry

      📗 Chemistry

      Stream: Science

      Description: Chemistry is the study of substances, their properties, structure, and the changes they undergo.

      Topics: Organic, Inorganic, Physical Chemistry, Periodic Table, Chemical Bonding

      Credits: 4

    3. Biology

      📕 Biology

      Stream: Science

      Description: Biology is the science of life and living organisms including their structure, function, growth and evolution.

      Topics: Cell Biology, Genetics, Ecology, Human Anatomy, Evolution

      Credits: 4

  2. 💼 Commerce Stream
    1. Accountancy

      📘 Accountancy

      Stream: Commerce

      Description: Accountancy deals with the process of recording, classifying and summarizing financial transactions.

      Topics: Journal, Ledger, Balance Sheet, Trial Balance, Cash Flow

      Credits: 4

    2. Economics

      📗 Economics

      Stream: Commerce

      Description: Economics is the study of how individuals and societies allocate scarce resources to satisfy unlimited wants.

      Topics: Micro Economics, Macro Economics, Supply & Demand, GDP, Inflation

      Credits: 4

    3. Business Studies

      📕 Business Studies

      Stream: Commerce

      Description: Business Studies covers the principles of business, management, marketing and finance.

      Topics: Management, Marketing, Finance, Business Environment, Entrepreneurship

      Credits: 4

  3. 🎨 Arts Stream
    1. History

      📘 History

      Stream: Arts

      Description: History is the study of past events, civilizations, cultures and their impact on the present world.

      Topics: Ancient History, Medieval History, Modern History, World Wars, Independence

      Credits: 4

    2. Geography

      📗 Geography

      Stream: Arts

      Description: Geography is the study of places, landscapes, environments and the relationships between people and their environments.

      Topics: Physical Geography, Human Geography, Maps, Climate, Natural Resources

      Credits: 4

    3. Political Science

      📕 Political Science

      Stream: Arts

      Description: Political Science is the study of government systems, political behavior, and political theory.

      Topics: Indian Constitution, Democracy, International Relations, Political Parties, Rights

      Credits: 4
